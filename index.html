Our Community

We are programmers and tech enthusiasts located in Greensboro, NC and surrounding areas. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned developer, we're here to connect, learn, and grow together.

How We Engage

We host monthly meetups for project sharing, tech discussions, networking, and learning. We embrace a broad spectrum of programming topics, spanning web development, game design, and much more.

Our Vision

We see Greensboro Devs as the go-to hub for local tech enthusiasts to come together, learn, and be creative. We aim to create a thriving, inclusive community where we can share our passion for programming.
